Whether to Buy or Rent

Know about short & long term impact on you and your finances. Use calculator to get an estimate. But also include:

  • Potential tax savings on purchase
  • Appreciation factors and house updates
  • Related ongoing costs, taxes of home ownership
    • Your Financial Readiness

    Includes more than having a strong income. You need to evaluate:


  • Your credit worthiness
  • Your ability to put money down on the home and handle closing costs
  • The documentation you can provide to support your loan application
  • Your ability to get pre-approved affects whether you are viewed as a strong buyer during negotiations
    • Decide on Type of Home You Want

    Home buying often involves compromises. It’s not usually possible to get the perfect home in the perfect location. Here are some issues you’ll need to address:

  • Style of home for your family
  • Property taxes, insurance and HOA costs
  • The condition of the home, yard space, square footage.
    • Your Preferred Location

    Will have a big impact on your lifestyle.
    You’ll need to choose a general location and then evaluate how well different neighborhoods meet your family preferences. Consider:

  • Commute to work, schools and shopping
  • Evaluate location during night and day times. Get to know at street level, talk to residents
  • Local real estate trends and developments.

    • Mortgage You’d Prefer

  • Bank loans vs. independent mortgage lenders

    - Cash-back or value added services

    A True Guide to buying a house in the San Francisco Bay Area

    Don’ts:
    - Fall in love with a listing without finding out what the block is like. In compact San Francisco, neighborhoods can change drastically within few blocks
    - Try to speed things along by skipping the inspection. With heavy rains, termites and seismic activity, there are plenty of ways Bay Area houses can suffer unseen damage
    - Let the price tag on a house overwhelm you. Always check with your mortgage lender
    Do's:
    - Write a letter telling sellers why you love their home.
    - Get pre-approved for a mortgage before you make your offer.
